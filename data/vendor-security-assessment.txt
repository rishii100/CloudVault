Vendor Security Assessment Questionnaire

Prepared for: CloudVault Systems
Date: March 2026

Instructions: Please answer each question below based on your organization's policies and practices.

1. What is the full legal name of your organization and in which country are you incorporated?

2. Describe your data encryption practices for data at rest and data in transit.

3. What compliance certifications does your organization currently hold (e.g., SOC 2, ISO 27001, HIPAA)?

4. Describe your access control mechanisms, including role-based access and multi-factor authentication.

5. What is your incident response process in the event of a security breach or data leak?

6. How do you manage and rotate encryption keys? Do you support customer-managed keys?

7. Describe your disaster recovery and business continuity plans, including RTO and RPO targets.

8. What is your uptime SLA and how do you ensure high availability across regions?

9. How do you handle vulnerability management, penetration testing, and bug bounty programs?

10. Describe your employee security training and background check policies.

11. What are your data backup frequency and retention policies?

12. How do you assess and manage third-party vendor security risks?
